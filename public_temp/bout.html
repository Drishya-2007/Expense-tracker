About Expense Tracker


Expense Tracker is a simple web application designed to help users track their daily income and expenses in an organized way.

Managing money can be difficult, especially when expenses are not recorded. This application helps users understand their spending habits and encourages better financial planning.

What You Can Do here?

  • Add income and expenses
  • Track daily spending
  • View total income and savings
  • Use on both mobile and desktop

Why use this website?

  • Helps you understand where your money goes
  • Keeps expenses organized
  • Simple and easy to use
  • No unnecessary features or confusion

Who should use it?

  • Students managing pocket money
  • Working professionals tracking monthly expenses
  • Anyone who wants better control over personal finances

Our goal is to make personal finance management simple, clear, and accessible for everyone.
