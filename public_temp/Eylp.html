User's guide

How to use this website?

  1. Select the Date
    2. Choose the date for which you want to record your expense.

  2. Enter Expense Name
    3. Type what you spent money on (for example: Food, Travel, Books, Recharge).

  3. Enter Amount Spent
    4. Enter the amount you spent in rupees (₹).

  4. Add Expense
    5. Click the “Add Expense” button to add your expenses one by one.

  5. Finish
    6. Click DONE after entering all your expenses for the day.

  6. Calculates
    7. Calculates your expenses on daily basis and shows your expenses for a month and a year.

Tips

  1. Enter expenses daily for better tracking
  2. Use clear names for expenses
  3. Check your expenses regularly to improve spending habits
